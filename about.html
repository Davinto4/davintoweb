About Chimdindu Macdonald (a.k.a Davinto)

It all began in September 2024. Driven by an insatiable curiosity about how websites work, Chimdindu Macdonald — known online as Davinto — started his journey into the world of web development. With no teacher or formal guide, he chose the path of self-learning.

Late nights, broken code, and endless debugging sessions marked the start of this incredible journey. At first, he failed to create even the simplest website. But he never gave up. Failure only made him more determined. Through consistent practice and relentless pursuit of knowledge, Chimdindu transformed from a beginner into a confident full-stack web developer.

Now, he proudly presents DavintoWeb — a hub where creativity meets functionality. From simple landing pages to full web platforms, DavintoWeb is a testament to the power of perseverance, vision, and self-taught skills.

Every project he delivers is polished with precision, animated with passion, and built for performance.
