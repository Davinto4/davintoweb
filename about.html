About DavintoWeb

My name is Chimdindu Macdonald, widely known online by my pseudonym Davinto. My journey into the tech world began in September last year, fueled by curiosity and a burning desire to understand how websites are built and work.

I started with nothing but determination and self-teaching. I scoured YouTube videos, read tech blogs, and practiced building websites from scratch. In the beginning, I failed countless times — my code broke, my layouts were terrible, and my ideas often didn’t translate into real functioning sites.

But I didn’t quit. I stayed up late, experimenting and debugging for hours. Over time, things started to click. I became well-grounded and confident in web development — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, hosting, and more.

Eventually, all that hard work and sleepless nights led to the creation of DavintoWeb — a full-service platform to build clean, modern websites for clients, friends, and anyone with a vision.

“I didn’t wait for the perfect time. I built my skills during the imperfect ones.”

From total beginner to creator of a real web service — the journey wasn’t smooth, but it was worth it. And it’s just the beginning.
