About DavintoWeb

I’m Chimdindu Macdonald, known online as Davinto. I'm a self-taught web developer passionate about building colorful, modern, and powerful websites for businesses, individuals, and projects.

My tech journey began from scratch — no formal training. Just pure curiosity, perseverance, and countless failed attempts that taught me everything I know today.

My Journey Into Tech

September 2024

I became curious and deeply inquisitive about how websites worked. With no background, I made the decision to explore web development.

October - December 2024

Started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript completely on my own. It was fully self-taught through tutorials, articles, and online communities.

Early 2025

Attempted building multiple websites but encountered countless errors and frustrations. Despite this, I remained relentless and kept learning.

Mid 2025

Finally succeeded in creating functional, beautiful websites. I became grounded in both frontend and backend development and built the DavintoWeb brand.
